# KPIs Selection for Business Insight

## Sales Performance (V-series)

| KPI ID   | KPI Name        | Description                                      | Use
|----------|----------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| KPI_V_1  | Total Sales    | Total number of cars sold                        | Dashboard
| KPI_V_2  | Sales by Year  | Analysis of sales trends over time               | Dashboard + KPIs selection     |
| KPI_V_3  | Sales by Model | Comparison across different car models           | Dashboard + KPIs selection     |

## Revenue Analysis (R-series)

| KPI ID   | KPI Name              | Description                                      |Use                      |
|----------|----------------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| KPI_R_1  | Total Revenue        | Revenue = Base Price × Sales Volume              |Dashboard                  |
| KPI_R_2  | Revenue by Model     | Revenue generated by each car model              |Dashboard + KPIs selection |
| KPI_R_3  | Revenue by Region    | Revenue distribution across regions              |KPIs selection             |
| KPI_R_4  | Average Car Price    | Average selling price of cars                    |Dashboard                  |
| YOY      | Average Car Price    | Year Over Year                                   |Dashboard                  |

## Product Analysis (P-series)

| KPI ID   | KPI Name                | Description                                      |Use                   |
|----------|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| KPI_P_1  | Sales by Fuel Type     | Distribution of sales by fuel type               |KPIs selection
| KPI_P_2  | Sales by Color         | Distribution of sales by car color               |Dashboard + KPIs selection|
| KPI_P_3  | Sales by Horsepower    | Analysis of sales by horsepower range            |Dashboard + KPIs selection|
